About Me

I'm a junior at UT Austin, studying Informatics with a focus on UX Design. I'm also a student-athlete on the Texas Men's Lacrosse team, which has taught me discipline, collaboration, and how to balance competing priorities.

I started my own business, Hill Country Pressure Wash, which gave me hands-on experience in building something from the ground up. I've also worked as an Analytics Intern at Young Life Studio 41, where I learned to pair data with creativity to tell meaningful stories.

What drives me is creating solutions that actually help people. I value both results and the relationships that make work meaningful. I'm curious, determined, and always learning.

Outside of school and work, my faith is important to me. I love being outdoors, traveling, and staying active.
